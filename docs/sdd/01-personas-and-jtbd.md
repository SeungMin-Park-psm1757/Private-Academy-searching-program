# Personas and JTBD

## Research Targets

- Parents: 8 to 12 interviews
- Academy operators: 4 to 6 interviews
- Platform operators or counselors: 2 to 3 interviews

## Parent Persona

### JTBD

- When I have limited time and many academy options, help me narrow to a small
  list I can trust.
- When schedule conflicts are likely, show me only options that realistically
  fit my child's timetable.
- Before I contact an academy, explain why it matches and what tradeoffs remain.

### Critical Questions

- Why is choosing an academy hard today?
- What creates distrust before final selection?
- Which criteria are deal-breakers?
- In what order do location, budget, style, and schedule matter?

## Academy Persona

### JTBD

- Let me keep my branch information accurate without high operational overhead.
- Make it easy to publish classes, schedules, and seat data.
- Help me receive qualified leads instead of low-intent traffic.

### Critical Questions

- Which fields change most often?
- Which update flow feels most annoying today?
- Which lead information is enough to follow up effectively?

## Platform Operator Persona

### JTBD

- Review risky academy changes before they affect recommendations.
- Recover from bad data changes without losing the audit trail.
- Investigate abnormal behavior or disputes quickly.

### Critical Questions

- Which fields are considered high risk?
- Which actions require approval, dual control, or MFA?
- What evidence is needed during incident review?

## Output From Discovery

Discovery must produce these artifacts before major implementation:

- Selection criteria list
- Rejection criteria list
- Trust criteria list
- Ranked priority of location, price, schedule, and teaching style
- Language patterns parents use in free-text requests
